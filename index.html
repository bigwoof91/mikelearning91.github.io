What is MOODU?

Snap your photo and MOODÜ will analyze your facial expression, identify your mood and suggest things you can do. Based on your mood and interests MOODÜ will find shopping deals of the day, give you quotes & excerpts to lift you up, give you music playlists, find special events & recreational activities and more. MOODÜ understands you and tries to help!

Find Your Fun

Find fun things to do based on your mood. and when & where the "happening" is happening.

Explore New Things

Find new and coming activities based on your interests. In this day and age there's always fun events, sports gatherings and recreational activies. MOODÜ helps you find them!

Be Your Best

Feeling down? Just show MOODÜ and you'll get suggestions that may help you be the best you that you can be. Music suggestions, funny videos, inspiring quotes and excerpts... MOODÜ's got your back!

Any Time, Anywhere

Whip out your phone, open up your laptop, snap a phot of you, show MOODÜ how you feel, and get quick suggestions on what you can do based on your mood. Find your fun - any time anywhere.
